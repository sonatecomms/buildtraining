# BUILD · Target List (50 schools)

> The sales pipeline for BUILD, Northeast Ohio. All 50 are pre-skinned in the demo, so every one renders in its own colors the moment you open the skinner (demo bar → palette icon). Pricing sheet: `build-pricing.md`. Prepared Jul 21, 2026.

## How to work this

- **Buyer:** head football coach or head of strength & conditioning first (they feel the pain), athletic director second (they hold the budget). Get the coach to want it, let them pull the AD in.
- **Priority order:** private/parochial schools (real S&C budgets and staff) → large publics with strong football → everyone else.
- **The math:** 24 schools at the standard $2,000 rate is $48k/year. Plan on working the whole list to net that, since not every demo closes. First cohort should get the $1,500 founding rate; those are your reference logos.
- **Radius honesty:** "built ten minutes away" is literally true for Tier 1 and mostly true for Tier 2. For the Stark marquee programs it becomes "built in your backyard, in Ohio." Still a local story, just a longer drive.
