The problem

The weight room still runs on paper.

Printed sheets
Percentage charts and workout cards, re-printed every cycle, lost by Wednesday.
The whiteboard
The program lives on a wall. It gets erased weekly and only exists inside the building.
The max board
A poster or spreadsheet, retyped by hand every test day, out of date the rest of the year.
The group text
Workouts and changes go out by text and get buried under everything else.
The summer blind spot
June and July decide the season, and there is no way to know who actually trained.
Apps that don't fit
Coaching apps are built for personal trainers and priced per client. Nothing about them is team.

Every one of these is a re-key or a guess, and the only person who can see the whole program is the coach who wrote it.

Your side of it

What the coach gets

A roster built for a team
Every athlete carries positions and a class year. Filter by position, unit, or grade in one tap.
Bulk programming
Filter to the O-line, or the whole offense, and assign weeks of training to all of them in one pass.
Real program structure
Straight sets, supersets, circuits, and coach notes, built by drag and drop, week by week.
An AI assistant
Describe the focus, equipment, and time you have, and get a complete session to review before you assign it.
The team scoreboard
Clean, bench, squat, deadlift, press, and the five-lift total, ranked live for the whole roster.
Training visibility
See who logged, when, how it felt, and who's slipping, without asking a single kid.
A line to every athlete
Built-in messaging per athlete, so feedback lands next to the workout instead of in a group text.
Exercise library
50+ movements with video form demos, plus anything your program does that we haven't named yet.
Percent-of-max calculator
Every logged top set feeds an estimated 1RM, and percentages are one tap away on the floor.
Their side of it

What your athletes get

The workout, not a PDF
Today's session on their phone: tap through sets, enter weight and reps, done.
A rest timer that coaches
Count-in, halfway call, and a 3-2-1 by voice, so rest is rest and work is work.
PRs without homework
Every top set updates their records and estimated 1RM automatically. Nobody fills in a chart.
Form on demand
Every movement links to a video demo, for the kid who won't ask twice.
A direct line to you
Chat with the coach inside the app, with unread badges so nothing gets missed.
Installs like an app
Straight to the home screen from the browser. No app store, no accounts to buy, works on whatever phone they have.
